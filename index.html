Our why

From Ground-Level Coordination to Macro-Level Framework Consulting

Volunteerism moves an estimated $167 billion through the U.S. economy every year — yet most of the organizations relying on it are running their programs on spreadsheets, tribal knowledge, and whoever happens to remember the CMS rule this quarter.

I started as a field coordinator, scheduling shifts and chasing paperwork. What I learned there — where programs actually break — is what shapes every audit and framework I build now: compliance and compassion aren't in tension. They're the same system, built well.
