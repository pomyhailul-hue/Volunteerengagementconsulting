About Volunteer Engagement Consulting

Building stronger volunteer systems for sustainable impact.

Our story, our values, and the founder behind the framework.

Volunteer programs are strategic assets — not acts of goodwill alone.

Volunteer Engagement Consulting was founded on the belief that volunteer programs are more than gestures of goodwill — they are strategic assets that strengthen organizations and communities. Yet too often, they rely on informal processes that limit their potential.

Drawing on years of hands-on experience in volunteer leadership and program development, VEC helps nonprofits, hospices, hospitals, and mission-driven organizations build resilient volunteer systems that are structured, compliant, and designed to grow.

Every framework, recommendation, and partnership is guided by one purpose: empowering organizations to create meaningful volunteer experiences that deliver lasting, measurable impact.

Build. Empower. Lead.

Hayat Nawd

Founder & Principal Consultant

Hayat brings a decade of ground-level volunteer coordination — translated into the governance frameworks that keep hospice, hospital, and nonprofit programs compliant, resilient, and human. Her practice began in the field, chasing paperwork and scheduling shifts, where she learned that compliance and compassion aren't in tension — they're the same system, built well.

Today she leads Volunteer Engagement Consulting's strategic partnerships across hospice, hospital, and corporate CSR programs nationwide, advising executive teams and boards on the governance structures that let volunteer programs scale without losing their culture.
